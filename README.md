# ITCS2550_RGaravaglia_Week1Assignment

![Formula Racecar](https://www.shutterstock.com/image-illustration/powerful-acceleration-generic-red-formula-260nw-2529063703.jpg)

#### This project is designed for you to take control of the wheel and have your hand at any track to see if you can post professional level laptimes at the track. You will get your chance to choose between 3 different styles of race car, each are built differently and go different speeds. You can store up to five sessions where it will take your name, the track, the vehicle type, each of your 3 laps, and the average of those 3 laps. Good Luck and Enjoy!
